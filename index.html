Mission

We provide the freshest, most delicious balut and penoy, honoring their cultural heritage, supporting sustainable local farming, engaging in community events, and innovating with respect for tradition.

Vision

To be the leading provider of authentic and high-quality balut and penoy, celebrating Filipino culinary heritage while promoting sustainable and ethical farming practices. We envision a world where traditional street food is enjoyed by all, bridging cultures and communities through shared flavors and experiences.
